Layoff Bingo

How to Play

  1. Find unplanned "All Hands" on your calendar
  2. Make a quick appraisal of personal finances
  3. Generate a board
  4. Mark the clichés you hear, hopefully before you are let go
%sveltekit.body%

In our era layoffs are just a fact of life and common. Whether it's to bring larger profits or stave off imminent collapse, real people are going to be impacted and have to deal with the stress of finding new work and taking care of themselves and their families.

What's also common are the words bosses use to talk about layoffs. So before the going into full stress mode, why not engage in some gallows humor and see just how many layoff clichés your leader is going to use. Share with your colleagues, generate your board, and the first one LETGO wins!
